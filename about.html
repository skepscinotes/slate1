About this site

Skeptical Science Notes

A biomedical scientist reading the literature in public.

Hi, I'm Iago — a biomedical scientist with a background in immunology. I started this site because... well... I needed to read more papers and needed an output to enforce it.

Skeptical Science Notes is me trying to hold myself accountable. A few journals a week, written up in plain language, with the kind of critical eye you'd apply in a lab meeting rather than a press release. No hype, no oversimplification — just the science, examined honestly. Maybe skeptically?

This ain't no news site nor textbook. It's more like a lab notebook that got a domain name. Some posts will be polished, maybe. Some will be half-formed thoughts I needed to get out of my head. All of them will be honest about what I know, what I don't, and what the literature actually says.

If you've made it this far, why? Go read stuff.
